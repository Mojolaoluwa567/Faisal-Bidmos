Our Purpose

We prioritize surpassing customer expectations by delivering exceptional value, adhering to strict timelines, and maximizing stakeholder returns. Our goal is to ensure that every interaction leads to meaningful impact and sustainable growth.
Vision: To become a foremost financial consulting boutique in Sub-Saharan Africa, recognized for our innovative solutions and commitment to excellence.
Mission: To align with international best practices in providing top-tier consulting services. With decades of experience in banking, finance, accounting, regulatory compliance, and enterprise risk management, we leverage our expertise to offer tailored solutions that meet the dynamic needs of our clients.

Our History

The audit and accounting firm FAISAL BIDMOS AND CO was established to premium audit and assurance services but has since grown to provide other range of services leveraging the experience of the partners in banking, tax, consulting, and other finance-related areas. We are known for providing quality services, which reflect the high standards we demand of ourselves. Our dedication to the three underlying principles of professionalism, responsiveness, and quality differentiates us from the rest. Our staff and management are professionals and are known to be people of integrity.

Our Drive

At the core of our operations is a commitment to exceeding customer expectations by consistently creating value, delivering results on time, and enhancing stakeholder returns. We are driven by the vision to be a leading financial consulting boutique in Sub-Saharan Africa, offering tailored solutions that reflect international best practices. Backed by years of expertise in banking, finance, accounting, regulatory compliance, and enterprise risk management, our mission is to leverage this wealth of experience to provide superior consulting services. Each project we undertake is fueled by our passion for excellence and our dedication to helping our clients thrive in today's dynamic financial landscape.
