Meet the Team

Our dynamic team combines a wealth of experience and a passion for excellence. We work together to provide tailored solutions that drive success and ensure your business’s financial health and transparency.

Mr Faisal Bidmos FCA

Team Lead / Managing Consultant

Faisal Bidmos is a Fellow of ICAN and Associate of CITN. He holds an MBA in Finance and a degree in Mathematics from University of Lagos. With over 20 years of professional experience, he has worked with Access Bank, Equity Bank, and Intercontinental Bank, holding roles in Strategy, MIS, and Financial Control. He is now the Managing Partner of Faisal Bidmos & Co., and a well-respected forensic auditor and advisor in financial Nigeria.

Mr Opeyemi Ogundele FCA

Associate Consultant

Opeyemi Ogundele is a Fellow of ICAN with over 18 years of experience in auditing, finance, and training. He has served in leadership roles at John O.G. Group, Foodco, and various audit firms. He also coordinates ICAN tuition at Uplink Professionals and runs his own firm, Moses Ogundele & Co. He is active in Christian missions and public speaking.

Mr Ganiu Muniru

Peer Review Partner

Mr. Muniru is an expert auditor for U.S. government-funded projects including those by USAID, UNICEF, and USCDC. With over two decades of experience, he has led audits for FHI 360, NEDC, and Catholic Caritas. He is an IFRS expert and chairman of the IFRS Implementation Team at Parker Randall Nigeria. He studied at the Polytechnic Ibadan and is a chartered accountant.

Ayodele Lasisi ACA

Associate Consultant

Ayodele Lasisi is a financial modelling and valuation analyst with a B.Sc in Accounting from OAU. He is an ACA and is pursuing further qualifications including CISA and CITN. AYO has experience in tax, data analysis, and internal controls.

Mr Olatunde Ibrahim ACA

Associate Consultant

Mr. Ibrahim is a seasoned accountant with decades of experience in NICON and A. Lawal & Co. He now runs his own firm, Complete Solution Consult, specializing in financial and forensic services. He is a chartered accountant and family man.
