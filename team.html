Meet the Team

Our dynamic team combines a wealth of experience and a passion for excellence. We work together to provide tailored solutions that drive success and ensure your business’s financial health and transparency.

Mr Faisal Bidmos FCA

Team Lead / Managing Consultant

Faisal Bidmos is a Fellow of ICAN and an Associate of CITN, with an MBA in Finance from the University of Lagos. He has over 20 years of experience in the Nigerian corporate space, including roles in banking and financial consulting.

Mr Opeyemi Ogundele FCA

Associate Consultant

With nearly two decades in finance and accounting, Opeyemi Ogundele is a Fellow of ICAN and a B.Sc. graduate from the University of Ibadan. He has expertise in auditing, tax, and consultancy.

Mr Ganiu Muniru

Peer Review Partner

An accredited auditor for USA government-funded projects, Ganiu Muniru has led numerous audits across Africa, including for USAID, UNICEF, and USADF.

Ayodele Lasisi ACA

Associate Consultant

A financial modeling and valuation analyst, Ayodele Lasisi has a degree in Accounting and is an associate of ICAN. He has deep expertise in tax, finance, and internal controls.

Mr Olatunde Ibrahim ACA

Associate Consultant

With vast experience in financial consultancy, Olatunde Ibrahim is a chartered accountant specializing in forensic auditing and fund recovery.
